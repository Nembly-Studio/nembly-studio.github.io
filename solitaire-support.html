Frequently asked questions

How do I cancel my free trial or subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Solitaire & Sudoku → Cancel Subscription. You can do this anytime, even during the free trial, and you won't be charged.

I bought a subscription but it's not showing on my new device. What do I do?

Open Solitaire & Sudoku and tap "Restore Purchases" in the app's settings or paywall screen. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

Will subscribing remove the ads?

Yes — subscribing removes ads and unlocks any additional features described in the app at the time of purchase.

I lost my game progress. Can it be restored?

Game progress is stored only on your device, not in the cloud. If you deleted the app, switched phones, or reset your device, unfortunately that progress can't be recovered.

Ads aren't loading, or the game feels slow. What should I check?

Make sure you have a stable internet connection (ads need internet even though gameplay itself works offline), then restart the app. If problems continue, try restarting your iPhone.
