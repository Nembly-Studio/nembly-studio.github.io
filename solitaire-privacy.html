Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Privacy Terms Support

Nembly Studio ("Nembly Studio," "we," "us," or "our") builds Solitaire & Sudoku and other small iOS apps. This Privacy Policy explains what information our apps handle, and how.

1. Information we collect

Solitaire & Sudoku is designed to work without an account. We do not ask you to register, and we do not knowingly collect your name, address, or other directly identifying information.

  • Gameplay data. Your game progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or transmit this data, and it is not backed up to any server we control.
  • Advertising. The app displays advertising provided by third-party advertising partners. These partners may collect technical identifiers, such as your device's advertising identifier (IDFA), approximate location, and app usage data, in order to deliver and measure ads. On iOS, this data collection is subject to Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework — you will be asked for permission before any cross-app tracking occurs, and you can change this choice anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Subscription and purchase information. If you start a free trial or subscribe, the purchase is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment card details. Apple shares limited transaction information with us, such as whether a subscription is active, so the app can unlock the features you've paid for.
  • Diagnostic data. Like most apps, we may receive aggregated, anonymized crash and performance data through Apple's standard developer tools. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. How we use this information

We use gameplay data only to run the app on your device. We use advertising-related data, where permitted, only to display and measure ads through our advertising partners. We use subscription information only to confirm your subscription status.

3. Sharing of information

We do not sell your information. Our advertising partners process ad-related data under their own privacy policies, and Apple processes all payments and subscription management under its own privacy practices, available at apple.com/legal/privacy.

4. Data retention

Gameplay data stays on your device until you delete the app. We retain subscription-status information only for as long as needed to provide the app's features.

5. Children's privacy

Solitaire & Sudoku is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertising shown to users who have not verified their age is served accordingly under our advertising partners' policies.

6. Your rights

If you have questions about the information we (or our advertising partners) hold, contact us at nemblystudio@outlook.com. If you're located in the EU/EEA, you may also have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app changes. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
