Frequently asked questions

How do I play Mancala?

Mancala is played by moving stones around the board, picking up all stones from one of your pits and dropping one in each pit going counter-clockwise, including your own store. The goal is to collect more stones in your store than your opponent.

Do I need to pay again on a new device?

No. Mancala is a one-time purchase tied to your Apple ID. If you get a new iPhone or reinstall the app, open the App Store and redownload Mancala — you won't be charged again.

I lost my game progress. Can it be restored?

Game progress is stored only on your device, not in the cloud. If you deleted the app, switched phones, or reset your device, unfortunately that progress can't be recovered.

Does Mancala need an internet connection?

No, Mancala works fully offline. You can play anytime, anywhere, with no internet required.

The app is behaving unexpectedly. What should I check?

Try restarting the app, and if that doesn't help, restart your iPhone and make sure you're on the latest version of the app from the App Store.
